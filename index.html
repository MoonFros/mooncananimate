Hello! I'm Moonfros

I've always believed that the space between frames is where the magic happens. Based in my cozy creative studio (mostly at night!), I specialize in hand-drawn, frame-by-frame animation that feels organic and full of life.

Inspired by Studio Ghibli, classic cartoons, and the quiet beauty of the night sky, my goal is to tell stories that resonate emotionally. Whether it's a 5-second loop or a narrative short film, I pour my heart into every line.
